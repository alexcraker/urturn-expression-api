Properties

.service

The service from wich this media come from. Currently 'youtube', 'vimeo' or 'dailymotion'.

.author

The author of the video

.duration

Duration of video in second.

.title

Title of video

.thumbnail

A thumbnail of the video

.views

The number of views of the video

.appData

The original media data, as return by the service
