UT.Sound

The sound object

Inherits from UT.Media

Properties

.service

The service from wich this media come from. Currently 'soundcloud' or 'itunes' .

.title

The title of the sound

.artist

The artist name

.cover

An image representing the sound

.artistCover

An image representing the artist

.soundCover

An image representing the sound album or track

.waveFormImage

The waveForm of the sound

.link

A link to the sound on the service

.appData

The original media data, as return by the service
